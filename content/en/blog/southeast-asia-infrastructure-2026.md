Southeast Asia is experiencing an unprecedented infrastructure construction boom. From Indonesia's new capital city to the Philippines' "Build Better More" program, the region presents massive opportunities for concrete suppliers and equipment buyers.

## Indonesia: The New Capital (IKN)

Indonesia's new capital city Nusantara in East Kalimantan requires:

- Multiple concrete batching plants for phased construction
- Estimated 5+ million m³ of concrete over 5 years
- Demand for HZS90-HZS180 plants

## Philippines: Build Better More

The Philippine government's infrastructure program includes:

- 150+ infrastructure projects under the new program
- Major highway, bridge, and airport expansions
- Strong demand for mid-range plants (HZS50-HZS90)

## Vietnam & Thailand

- Vietnam: North-South expressway, metro lines in Hanoi and HCMC
- Thailand: Eastern Economic Corridor, high-speed rail connecting three airports
- Both markets favor reliable, cost-effective Chinese equipment
