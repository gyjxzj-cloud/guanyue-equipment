## Overview

The HZS120 is a large-scale concrete batching plant capable of producing 120 m³ of high-quality concrete per hour. It is the go-to choice for major infrastructure projects including highways, airports, and large commercial developments.

## Key Features

- **JS3000 powerful mixer** — high output with consistent quality
- **Aggregate heating system** — optional for cold climates
- **Automatic slump control** — moisture sensor feedback
- **ERP integration** — production data management
- **Containerized shipping** — standard 40ft container fit

## Applications

- Highway and railway projects
- Airport runway construction
- Large commercial complexes
- Major infrastructure (dams, ports, bridges)
- Large ready-mix plants
