## Mobile Concrete Batching Plants

For projects that require frequent relocation — such as road construction, tunnel projects, and remote infrastructure — we offer mobile and containerized concrete mixing plants. These are pre-assembled in standard shipping containers and can be operational within 24 hours of arrival.

**Best for:** Road projects, remote sites, short-term projects

## Commercial Ready-Mix Stations

If you're setting up a commercial concrete supply business, our HZS75-HZS120 models are ideal. Complete with cement silos, admixture systems, and automated dispatch, we can help you build a turnkey ready-mix station.

**Best for:** Concrete suppliers, urban construction

## Large Infrastructure Solutions

For major projects like dams, ports, airports, and high-speed railways, our HZS120 and HZDS180 plants deliver the volume and reliability you need. We provide full project engineering, including foundation design, installation supervision, and commissioning.

**Best for:** Mega infrastructure, government projects

## Low-Cost Entry Solutions (Africa & SE Asia)

We understand budget constraints in emerging markets. Our HZS25 and HZS50 models offer exceptional value — factory-direct pricing with reliable performance. We also provide financing options and spare parts packages for first-time buyers.

**Best for:** Small contractors, startups, rural development
