## Overview

The HZS25 is a compact concrete batching plant designed for small-scale construction projects, road maintenance, and rural infrastructure. Its modular design makes it easy to transport and quick to install.

## Key Features

- **Compact footprint** — requires minimal foundation work
- **Modular structure** — easy disassembly and relocation
- **JS500 twin-shaft mixer** — ensures uniform mixing quality
- **PLC control system** — simple operation with manual backup
- **Low power consumption** — ideal for sites with limited power supply

## Applications

- Rural road construction
- Small housing projects
- Hydropower and irrigation projects
- Precast concrete products
- Emergency repair works
