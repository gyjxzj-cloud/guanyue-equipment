HZDS180 Concrete Mixing Plant

Overview

The HZDS180 is our flagship model, delivering 180 m³ of concrete per hour with dual mixer configuration. Built for mega-scale infrastructure projects, this plant represents the pinnacle of Chinese concrete batching technology.
