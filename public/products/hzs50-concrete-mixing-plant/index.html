HZS50 Concrete Mixing Plant

Overview

The HZS50 is one of our most popular models, offering the perfect balance between capacity and mobility. It is widely used in commercial concrete production and medium-sized construction projects across Southeast Asia and Africa.

Key Features

  • High mixing efficiency — JS1000 twin-shaft forced mixer
  • Automated batching — precise aggregate and cement weighing
  • Modular design — containerized structure for easy shipping
  • Optional mobile version — towable chassis available
  • Quick setup — operational within 3-5 days on site

Applications

  • Commercial ready-mix concrete stations
  • Medium housing and building projects
  • Road and bridge construction
  • Precast and pipe manufacturing
