HZS75 Concrete Mixing Plant

Overview

The HZS75 is designed for serious concrete production. With 75 m³/h output capacity and advanced control systems, it serves as the ideal solution for medium-to-large construction projects and commercial concrete stations.

Key Features

  • JS1500 twin-shaft mixer — robust and low maintenance
  • Independent metering system — high accuracy weighing (±1%)
  • Dust collection system — environmentally friendly operation
  • Central control room — comprehensive monitoring
  • Options for heating/insulation — cold climate operation

Applications

  • Large housing and commercial building projects
  • Highway and expressway construction
  • Medium-sized dams and bridges
  • Commercial concrete supply stations
