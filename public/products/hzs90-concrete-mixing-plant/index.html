HZS90 Concrete Mixing Plant

Overview

The HZS90 is a high-capacity concrete batching plant built for continuous, large-scale concrete production. It features dual mixers and fully automated control, making it a top choice for major infrastructure projects.

Key Features

  • JS2000 twin-shaft mixer — heavy-duty mixing with replaceable liners
  • Dual mixer available — for redundant operation
  • Full auto-control — one-person operation capability
  • High durability — wear-resistant mixing blades and liners
  • Centralized dust removal — meets environmental standards

Applications

  • Large infrastructure projects (bridges, tunnels)
  • High-rise building construction
  • Large commercial concrete stations
  • Precast concrete factories
